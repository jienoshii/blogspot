Our Mission

In Japan, many people think of private schools, study abroad, English schools, and cram schools when they want to raise children who can excel in the world without paying high tuition fees. However, these options may be difficult for those without financial means or living in less developed areas.

To address this, we have established a school that offers children new choices. They can learn programming and English simultaneously, and also make international friends through an online second school. Our aim is to provide children with globally applicable skills and opportunities for the future.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every child, regardless of financial circumstances or geographical location, has access to a world-class education that empowers them with globally relevant skills. Our commitment is to foster a generation of forward-thinking individuals who not only excel in programming and English proficiency but also thrive in an interconnected world. By leveraging innovative online education, we aspire to create a diverse community of students who seamlessly navigate cultural boundaries, develop critical thinking, and embrace opportunities for a successful and impactful future on a global scale.
